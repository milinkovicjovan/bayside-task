Hillsborough Permeable Pavers Contractor

Permeable pavers come in various colors and shapes that blend in with existing landscaping, creating a cohesive look. Additionally, they can be installed on slopes without the need for drainage pipes, which are often necessary when using traditional hardscaping.

Permeable pavers are a great solution for those looking to reduce their environmental impact in the long term. Not only do they help protect watersheds from harmful runoff, but they also require less maintenance and look great while doing it!

It is important to note that while these pavers reduce runoff and provide drainage control, it's best to pair them with other water management methods, such as rain gardens or bioswales, to maximize the benefits of permeable paving.

We use a method called "open grading" to install permeable pavers. This process involves using larger gravel pieces and a more “open” joint between the stones to allow for water to enter the soil beneath easily.

In comparison to conventional pavers, installing permeable pavers is relatively simple. In the majority of instances, an experienced Hillsborough paver contractor can complete the installation swiftly, allowing you to begin enjoying your outdoor space without delay.
