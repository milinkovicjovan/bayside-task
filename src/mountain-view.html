Mountain View Permeable Pavers Contractor

Permeable pavers are an ideal choice for homeowners who wish to create a beautiful and environmentally-friendly hardscaping solution. In addition to reducing runoff and protecting watersheds, permeable pavers can also provide additional benefits such as improved water filtration and better soil health. This is because when installed correctly with open grading, the water is exposed to fewer potentially hazardous contaminants and slowly drains through the base layers. This helps create a healthier balance between soil and water content, ultimately improving the overall health of your yard.

The cost of installation for permeable pavers can be more than traditional paving solutions, but they typically require less maintenance and repairs. Additionally, if designed correctly, permeable pavers can help to manage stormwater runoff, reduce flooding risk and even help protect against soil erosion.

Installing permeable pavers is relatively easy in comparison to conventional pavers. In most cases, an experienced Mountain View paver contractor can complete the installation quickly, allowing you to enjoy your outdoor space in no time.
