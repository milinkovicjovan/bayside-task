Dependable San Francisco Permeable Pavers Installation Company

Homeowners who want to reduce the environmental impact of their hardscaping projects are drawn to permeable pavers.

We install permeable pavers to promote the slow drainage of water through the base layers. This method of laying pavers is referred to as "open grading." We employ larger gravel pieces and more "open" spaces to promote the flow of water through the soil. Thus, the water is exposed to fewer potentially hazardous contaminants, reducing harmful runoff and safeguarding watersheds.

Although permeable pavers are typically more expensive to install, their installation requires less time. Typically, a skilled San Francisco paver contractor can complete the installation rapidly, allowing you to immediately begin enjoying your outdoor space. In addition, they do not need a separate drainage system, so their price is comparable to that of conventional concrete pavers.

Permeable pavers are an excellent eco-friendly option for hardscaping projects such as installing a patio, pool deck, or walkway.
