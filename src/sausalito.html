Sausalito Permeable Pavers Contractor

Traditional impermeable surfaces necessitate considerably more upkeep than permeable pavers. But with permeable pavers, you won't have any accumulated runoff, so there is no need for regular cleaning and drainage maintenance. As water flows through permeable pavers, it aerates and improves the soil below, making it healthier and more erosion-resistant.

We use the "open grading" method to install permeable pavers. The process involves using larger gravel and creating more open seams for more water passageways. By keeping the water on-site, permeable pavers reduce runoff and the chances of contaminating the water. In addition to being more eco-friendly than conventional hardscaping materials, permeable pavers help conserve soil moisture and reduce the need for rainwater management systems.

Permeable pavers are an aesthetically pleasing means of reducing the environmental impact of hardscaping and protecting watersheds. They are initially more expensive to install than conventional hardscaping materials, but they require less maintenance over time and can withstand extreme weather without losing their aesthetic appeal.

In comparison to conventional pavers, installing permeable pavers is relatively simple. Typically, an experienced Sausalito paver contractor can complete the installation expeditiously, allowing you to begin enjoying your outdoor space immediately.
