Santa Rosa Permeable Pavers Contractor

Permeable pavers captivate homeowners who want to reduce the environmental impact of their hardscaping projects.

The installation of permeable pavers is intended to facilitate the slow drainage of water through the base layers. This technique for laying pavers is called "open grading." We use larger gravel pieces and more "open" spaces to facilitate the flow of water through the soil. As a result, fewer potentially hazardous contaminants are exposed to the water, reducing harmful runoff and protecting watersheds.

Despite the fact that permeable pavers are typically more expensive to install, they require less time. Usually, a skilled Santa Rosa paver contractor can complete the installation quickly, allowing you to begin enjoying your outdoor space immediately. In addition, they don’t require a separate drainage system, so their cost compares to the standard concrete pavers.

Permeable pavers are an excellent eco-friendly choice for hardscaping projects like installing a patio, pool deck, or walkway.
