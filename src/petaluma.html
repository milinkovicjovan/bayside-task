Petaluma Permeable Pavers Contractor

Permeable pavers attract homeowners who wish to reduce the environmental impact of hardscaping.

Traditional impermeable surfaces require significantly more maintenance than permeable pavers. Since you won't deal with accumulated runoff when you install permeable pavers, there's no need for frequent cleaning and maintenance. The water that flows through permeable pavers also aerates and fertilizes the soil below, making it healthier and more resistant to erosion.

We install permeable pavers using the "open grading" technique. This involves using larger gravel or other material pieces to create passageways for water. This reduces runoff and improves water quality by keeping the water on-site. In addition to being more eco-friendly than conventional hardscaping materials, permeable pavers reduce the need for stormwater management systems and aid in soil moisture conservation.

Permeable pavers are an attractive way to reduce the environmental impact of hardscaping and safeguard watersheds. They are initially more expensive to install than conventional hardscaping materials, but they require less maintenance over time and can withstand extreme weather conditions while retaining their attractive appearance.

In comparison to conventional pavers, installing permeable pavers is faster. Typically, an experienced Petaluma paver contractor can complete the installation expeditiously, allowing you to begin enjoying your outdoor space immediately.
