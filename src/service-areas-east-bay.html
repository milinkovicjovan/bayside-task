East Bay Permeable Pavers Contractor

We install permeable pavers to promote the slow drainage of water. This method of laying pavers is referred to as "open grading." We employ larger gravel particles and more "open" spaces to promote water movement in the soil. Therefore, the water is exposed to fewer potentially hazardous contaminants, reducing harmful runoff and safeguarding watersheds.

Although permeable pavers are typically more expensive to install, they are typically installed more quickly. Typically, a skilled paver contractor in East Bay can complete the installation quickly, allowing you to immediately begin enjoying your outdoor space. In addition, they do not require a separate drainage system, so they are priced similarly to conventional concrete pavers.

Permeable pavers are an eco-friendly option for hardscaping projects such as installing a patio, pool deck, or walkway.
