Permeable Pavers Contractor in Berkeley

We install permeable pavers to promote the slow drainage of water. The method for laying pavers is called "open grading." To facilitate water movement in the soil, we employ larger gravel particles and more "open" spaces containing fewer particles. Thus, the water is exposed to fewer potentially hazardous contaminants, reducing contaminated runoff and safeguarding watersheds.

While permeable pavers are typically more expensive to install, they are quicker to install. Usually, a skilled Berkeley paver contractor can complete the installation quickly, allowing you to immediately begin enjoying your outdoor space. Moreover, they do not necessitate a separate drainage system in the majority of homes.

Permeable pavers are an eco-friendly option for hardscaping projects like installing a patio, pool deck, or walkway.
